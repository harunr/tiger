What is Vitality Drive?

Discovery Insure is committed to creating a nation of great drivers and encourage good driving through their driving programme, Vitality Drive.

Vitality Drive is Discovery Insure’s unique driver behaviour programme that rewards their clients for driving well, gives them access to a range of service and reward partners to help them become a better driver and stay safe on roads.

As a Vitality Drive member, you get up to 20% discount on purchases at Tiger Wheel & Tyre.

A Tiger Wheel & Tyre technician will assist you with:

  • Downloading the Discovery Insure app if you haven’t done so already.
  • Registering on the Discovery Insure app.
  • Activating and linking your Vitality Drive Sensor to the app and placing the device in your vehicle.
  • Linking your Vitality Drive card to your plan to earn fuel cash back.
Note: If you need a replacement Vitality Drive card, you can pick one up at Tiger Wheel & Tyre.
